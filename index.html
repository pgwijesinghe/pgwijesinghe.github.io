Hello! I'm Pubudu Wijesinghe

I am a Graduate Student Researcher at the LevyLab at University of Pittsburgh. I am a Graduate student at LevyLab at University of Pittsburgh, USA. My major background is in Physics and I have minored in Mathematics and Computer Science. My research experience spans over Optics, Nanoelectronics and Quantum Information.

My research looks at Non-linear optical behavior of LAO/STO heterostructures which can then be utilized to talk to different materials such as Graphene and Graphene Nanoribbons to probe their properties.

In addition to research, my passion lies in Science Communication. I have 3+ years of tutoring experience (Physics and Physics Education) at the University of Colombo, Sri Lanka and the University of Pittsburgh, USA.
